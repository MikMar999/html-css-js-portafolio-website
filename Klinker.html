KLINKER

Costumes for Michiel Teeuw's performance, (2021)

KLINKER is a research project into the construction of words, tiles, letters, and other parts. Through the object of a “klinker” (Dutch for both brick and vowel), Teeuw zooms in on the Dutch language and the Groningen surroundings. My role in this project was to design the costumes and jewelry for the show, as well as for the afterparty where Teeuw performs and reveals the final look.

  • Image 1: A performer walks across a field in the Dutch countryside, wearing a full white overall costume.
  • Image 2: Five performers engage in a group meditation, showing how the costume shifts and changes with movement.
  • Image 3: As the arms move, the orange padding creates shapes that translate into a visual language.
  • Image 4: Two performers stand, one showing the front of the costume and the other the back, while a third bows on the grass.

KLINKER Jewelry

Jewelry for Michiel Teeuw's performance, (2021)

These jewelry pieces were created for KLINKER. Each piece was crafted using only discarded materials, including plastic left overs and metal scraps. The aim was to continue the KLINKER futura minimalistic aesthetic explore asymmetry through form while working sustainably.

  • Image 1: A mismatched pair of earrings. One is minimalistic, a simple hanging line. The other is more complex, built from an industrial chain link holding an inverted translucent U-shaped piece.
  • Image 2: Another mismatched set, both in orange plastic with chain links. Their shapes and alternating constructions complement one another.
  • Image 3: A fully metal set. One earring hangs as a straight line, while the other forms an asymmetrical upside-down U.
  • Image 4: An asymmetrical earring made from translucent blue plastic, designed to complement its pair.
  • Image 5: Side profile view highlighting the glass-like blue earring.

GAG After

Costumes for Afterparty (2021)

GAG is a queer-oriented club night that challenges and redefines the boundaries of contemporary music. Organized by a small group of DJs and performers, it creates a space for experimentation, play, and expression.

  • Image 1: Harness and top designed for GAG, created to complement the bottoms of the KLINKER costume.
