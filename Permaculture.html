2. Ad Hoc

About the Project

Ad Hoc is a documented experiment applying permaculture design within an urban environment. The project explored how to ethically reconstruct a rented house, test self-sufficiency, and assess feasibility in a city context.

  • Image 1: Poster showing the project design with raised beds, water collection gutters, and a greenhouse.

  • Image 3: Side view of the vegetable containers showing companion plants growing on the rooftop.
  • Image 4: Compost bin with earthworms and potworms, used to dispose of my food scraps and later used to nourish the vegetable bins.
