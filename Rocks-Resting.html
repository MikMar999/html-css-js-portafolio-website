Rocks Resting

Costumes for Michiel Teeuw's performance, (2022)

This performance placed three large rocks in the city center as a temporary resting space for five participants. Amid the bustling, snowy streets, they cared for each other and rested, creating an oasis of silence while confronting exhaustion from forces like ableism, xenophobia, illness, and work precarity. Presented by Kunstpunt Groningen and Eurosonic Noorderslag.
